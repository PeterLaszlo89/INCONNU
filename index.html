Story Introduction

The plot takes place not in the very distant future, on a continent of Earth. Climate change has brought such extreme conditions, making a planet largely uninhabitable. First, the country’s agricultural economy went bankrupt and then destroyed the world economy, meanwhile greenhouse effect has led to the depletion of the ozone layer and the ice sheets thus melted permanently,with a rise in water levels followed by disasterous floods in several of the coastal towns. Tsunami and tornado disasters followed one another. Mankind has used all the technological advantages to save the economy, but to no avail. The remaining population fled to a continent. In the chaos, many tried to create power for themselves, and eventually two people succeeded. These two people were driven by conflicting interests. A war broke out for even greater power in the name of unification and the war led nowhere.
Eventually, the survived remaining population halved the continent and than two countries were born. In the country where the story begins,there's the communist regime and the other has democracy. Many people who disagree with the communist party and the system ,they are trying to escape to another country. The two countries have the opportunity to provide assistance for each other ,but peace between the two, has not been reached.
And this is where our story begins...

Narrative

To remain human in captivity, to “become a survivor”. Humanity, love for another fellow human beings.
Equality, the desire for freedom and its elementary indestructibility. Paradigm shift within the individual and reassessment of it.
